Welcome to Faozio Kennels

At Faozio Kennels, we provide the best care for your beloved pets. Our experienced staff is dedicated to ensuring a comfortable and enjoyable stay for your furry friends.

Some of our popular and loved breeds!
The French Bulldog: a compact, affectionate breed with bat-like ears and expressive eyes. A delightful companion known for its playful personality and charming appearance.
The German Shepherd Dog, a versatile and intelligent breed, is highly regarded for its strong and agile physique, loyalty, and suitability for various roles, including working and service dogs.
The Golden Retriever Dog, with its friendly and gentle nature, is cherished for its lustrous golden coat and adaptable personality. They bring joy as therapy dogs and loyal family companions.
The Labrador Retriever, a beloved breed, is known for its friendly and outgoing nature. With their versatile and athletic build, they make excellent family pets and are highly adaptable.
